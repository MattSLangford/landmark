Give to Landmark Church

Landmark Church is a local, family-led church that operates entirely through voluntary giving. We are not a top-down organization with denominational funding. Every Celebrate Recovery gathering, kids lesson, local outreach, and counseling appointment exists because people in Acadiana believe in this mission.

When you give, you are resourcing prayer meetings, discipleship, benevolence, and the day-to-day ministry that keeps our doors open all week long.

Thank you for believing in what God is doing at Landmark. Below are the simplest ways to partner financially; if you ever have questions or want to discuss unique gifts, we’re happy to talk.
