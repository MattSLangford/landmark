We live stream all Sunday services at 10am CST in Full 1080p HD across many devices. We also offer chat (during live streams) and select archives. You can find your best way to watch below.

In Our App

Download the Church Center app on your iPhone or Android device to access our live stream, archives (video and audio), and much more.

In Your Browser

Watch right in your browser on virtually any device. Access our live stream, archives, and more.

On Your TV

Watch our live stream directly on your Smart TV or Streaming Box by using YouTube app. Only live services are available via Youtube.

Our Podcast

You can listen to the audio of Landmark Sermons by subscribing to the Landmark Church podcast in your preferred podcast listening app. Just search for "Landmark Church" and look for our logo.
