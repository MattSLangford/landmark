Visit Us

We can’t wait to welcome you to Landmark Church! Whether you’re visiting for the first time or you’ve been part of our family for years, you’ll find a warm, encouraging community ready to worship and grow together. Join us Sundays at 10 am for heartfelt praise, passionate worship, and a life-changing message. Come as you are—there’s a place here for everyone, and we’re saving a seat just for you!
