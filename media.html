Contact Landmark Church

Follow the directions below to submit notes, presentation files, graphics, and directions to the Landmark media team.

Send an email to media at landmark lafayette dot com & matt at landmark lafayette dot com with the following information:

  • Date Speaking
  • Title of Sermon
  • Attach Notes as a PDF
  • Attach Images and Videos*
  • Attach Presentation Files if Necessary**
  • Include any additional instructions for sound and media teams.

*Images are best when in landscape orientation. Many videos will not work due to copyright laws.

**If attaching a presentation file, make sure you've communicated with the media team to understand proper file types, sizes, fonts, and more. There are many restrictions.
