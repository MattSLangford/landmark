Giving - Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage my giving?

You can manage all details of your giving through Planning Center. You're able to manage your recurring giving, donation methods, see giving history, access donor statements, see pledges and progress, and much more.

Will I receive a giving statement?

You will receive a receipt for every donation immediately. You'll also receive a yearly statement every January for your tax purposes.

How secure is giving to Landmark Church?

Very secure. Our payment processors, used by tens of thousands of businesses and churches, use the highest forms of security available.

Are donations tax deductible?

Yes. All donations to Landmark Church are fully tax deductible.

What is the deadline for annual donations?

Cash and check contributions must be received or postmarked by December 31st to be included on your giving statement for that year. Online transactions must be initiated by 11:59 pm ET on December 31st to be included.

Can I mail in donations?

Absolutely! Send it to PO Box 62833, Lafayette, LA 70596.
