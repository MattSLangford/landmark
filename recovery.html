Celebrate Recovery

What Is Celebrate Recovery?

Celebrate Recovery is a Christ-centered, 12-step recovery program for anyone struggling with hurts, habits, or hang-ups. It combines biblical teaching with proven recovery principles to offer a safe, grace-filled community where real healing happens.
