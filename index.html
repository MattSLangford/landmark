SafeHouse is a 501c3 non-profit organization which offers support to those who are struggling with poverty, substance abuse, and homelessness in the greater Lafayette, Louisiana area.

SafeHouse offers vital services that include housing (for recovering addicts and sexual abuse victims), hot meals, and clothing. We also offer access to Celebrate Recovery (a 12 step recovery program), counseling and emotional support systems.

Our goal is to open 10 sober living houses in the next three years.
