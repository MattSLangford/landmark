COVID-19 UPDATE

With Louisiana entering Phase 1 of the COVID-19 response, we have began the process of re-opening certain aspects of Landmark Church. Beginning on May 31, we will have services open to the public with restrictions.

In order to attend in person, you must pre-register (free). To accomodate more people, we will be having two services every Sunday (9am and 11am). We will also continue to live stream every Sunday service and Wednesday devotion.

Celebrate Recovery and SafeHouse will be operating on a limited basis moving forward. Those details will be communicated soon.
