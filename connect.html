Connect with Landmark Church

At Landmark, we want everyone to know there is a place for them. We have many opportunities for you to be involved and connect with like minded people.

Check things out below. If you dont see anything that interests you, let us know. We will do our best to remedy that!

Groups

Start or Join a semester based Life Group or Freedom Group to stay connected with people just like you.

Celebrate Recovery

Everyone struggles with something. Connect with others attempting to overcome hurts, habits, and hangups.

SafeHouse

A fully independent non-profit organization run by Landmark, Safehouse is focused on feeding and reaching those in need.

First Wednesday

On the first Wednesday of each month at 6:30pm, we gather over a free meal and a short activity to build stronger connections with our church family. Everyone is invited! Kid friendly atmosphere.

Growth Track

If you're new to Landmark, we offer an introductory course that examines our beliefs and walks you through life at Landmark Church.

Students and Youth

We have frequent events, both socially and Biblically based, for our youth. Bible studies, game night, eating events, and more.

Kids

We love our kids! Excellent childcare with a Biblical backing is provided during all Sunday Services and Celebrate Recovery.

And more!

From gumbo cookoffs to tea parties to market days, Landmark always provides fun ways to connect with others. Check our calendar for what's coming up.
