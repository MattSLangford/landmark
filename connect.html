Ways to Connect

Landmark is unlike most churches. We believe true disciples of Christ should be willing to get their hands dirty by doing things the world truly needs.

We believe Jesus Christ brings personal transformation so strong it can transform the world. We live that out by being present in people’s darkest places and hours, coming from broken homes, destroyed marriages, and difficult pasts—helped by Jesus and each other to overcome the darkness.
