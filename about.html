Our Beliefs

There is One God, the Creator of the universe and everything in it including humanity. When Adam and Eve sinned in the Garden, we needed a Savior. God became flesh, died in our place, and three days later rose from the dead—defeating hell and the grave. This is the gospel.

Heaven is the eternal reward for those who hear and obey the gospel; hell is reserved for those who refuse.

Grace opens the door to salvation; faith is our response to that grace. Confession acknowledges our dependence on Him. Repentance is our decision to turn from sin, and baptism in His Name brings remission of sins. The baptism of the Holy Spirit is the promise given to all believers. We endure in faith until our heavenly Father welcomes us home.

“The Spirit of the Lord is upon me, because He has anointed me to preach the gospel to the poor; He has sent me to heal the brokenhearted, to proclaim liberty to the captives and recovery of sight to the blind, to set at liberty those who are oppressed; to proclaim the acceptable year of the Lord.”—Luke 4:18-19 (NKJV)

Jesus is coming again—to resurrect believers, rapture living disciples, and establish His millennial kingdom.

We believe God still heals and works miracles today, proven not just in Scripture but in our personal experience.
